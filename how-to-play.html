How to play

Instructions for the quiz

Hello! Welcome to this quiz page! Let's dive right in and explain how the game works: This is a quiz consisting of four different categories: geography, science, music and animals. You have to answer to all the questions of one category before you can move on to the next one! To start a game, simply click on one of the four category buttons.

Confirm and go to the next question

Once you've read a question, you have to select one of the four answer options. Once you've selected your answer, you have to click first on the "Confirm" button, and once you're ready to move on to the next question you can click on the "Next question" button.

Life Points and Score

At the top of the page, you can see your Score and your Life points. If you answer a question correctly, your score will increase. If your answer is incorrect, your life points will diminish, so be careful! Once your life points reach 0, The game is over and you will have to start again. Can you beat all four categories with 5 life points?
